Frequently Asked Questions

What is there to do in the City?

The City offers a variety of activities and attractions for residents and visitors alike. You can explore the local parks, visit museums, attend events, or simply enjoy the vibrant atmosphere. Don't forget to check out our upcoming events!

What do I need to do to prep for a visit?

You can visit The City by following the guidelines provided on our Visit page. Make sure to pack any necessary items, including offerings to our local gods!

Where do I find my god's altar?

You can find your god's altar in the designated areas of The City. Each god has a specific location where their altar is situated. If you are unsure, please contact the City Hall for assistance.

Is the City Dangerous?

No! The City is a safe and welcoming place for all. However, it is important to follow the rules and guidelines to ensure a safe and enjoyable experience.

I think you forgot a god!

We likely did not, but if you think we did, please contact us here and we will look into it. Please remain inside your home until the matter is resolved. Do not leave. Do not trust the thing in the light, it is trying to lure you outside.
